About the fields
Create New Invoice

Click to display on screen the fields required to create a new invoice.

New Invoice Start date

The new invoice start date i.e. the date from which you want to invoice.

New Invoice End date

The new invoice end date i.e. the date up to which you want to invoice.

Create

Click to create the new invoice for the period defined by the start and end date.

Record Payment

Click to display on screen the fields required to record a payment.

Invoice

Select the invoice Reference for which you are recording payment.

Date

Select the date you are recording the payment.

Amount

The amount received in payment for the messages sent.

List of billing costs and balances

A list of your billing costs and balances is shown and includes: Reference, Start Date, End Date, the number of Sent Messages, the Total Cost and Balance Outstanding. The Total Balance is also displayed.

Use this screen to review billing history for SMS messaging, create invoices and record payments.

How do I ...

Create an invoice?
  • Click Create New Invoice.
  • Enter the Start Date and End Date for the period you want covered by the invoice.
  • Click Create.
Record a payment?
  • Click Record Payment.
  • Select the Invoice for which you are entering payment
  • Enter the Date and the Amount that is being paid.
  • Click Enter Payment.
