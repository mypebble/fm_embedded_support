Dashboard

The dashboard provides a way of accessing things quickly. You can see a high-level view of transactions over a time period, search account balances or transactions, see breakdowns of cash in hand, and whatâ€™s in your bank accounts, and see a breakdown of accounts, names and transactions in Fund Manager.

Transactions

See a high-level view of transactions over a time period.

  • Enter a receipt or record a payment by clicking Receipt or Payment on the Transactions chart.
  • Select different transactions from the More Transactions drop-down list.

Tip: You can also select these options from the Transactions tab.

Reports
  • Click Search Account Balances to search your account balances, or Search Transactions to search your transactions.

Tip: You can also select these options from the Reports tab.

Bank & Cash

See a banking summary with a breakdown by cash in hand, cheques in hand, and the amounts in your various bank accounts.

  • Click Bank Receipts to bank receipts or Reconcile to reconcile a bank account.

Tip: You can also select these options from the Bank & Cash tab.

Organisation Summary

See a breakdown of the number of accounts, names and transactions within Fund Manager.

  • Click Names or Manage Names to manage the names set up within Fund Manager.
  • Click Accounts or Manage Accounts to manage the accounts set up within Fund Manager.
  • Click Transactions to search the transactions in Fund Manager.

Tip: You can also select Manage Names or Manage Accounts options from the Administration tab.
