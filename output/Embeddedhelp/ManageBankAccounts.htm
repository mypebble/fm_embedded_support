Manage Bank Accounts

About the fields
List of bank accounts

A list of all bank accounts in the system. For each bank, the Code, Account, Balance and Status (whether it is in use or not) is shown.

Note: All bank accounts are displayed, including those that are out of use.

Filter

Enter a character or characters in the account you want to find and the list of displayed accounts adjusts accordingly to show a shorter list to choose the from.

Note: The filter works by displaying all accounts which contain the character or characters you have entered.

Use this screen to manage your bank accountsAn account held with a bank for purposes of managing your funds, e.g. current account, savings account..

Note: A current account with code CURR and name Current Account is created for you by default. This is the most frequently used account in most schools. You can change the account name of this account but you should leave the code as CURR to ensure that it stays at the top of the alphabetical list of bank accounts.

How do I ...

Create a bank account?
  • Click Create Bank Account and follow the instructions on the Create New Account screen.
Amend a bank account?
  • Click the hyperlinked Account name to display the Update Account screen.
Remove a bank account?
  1. Click the hyperlinked Account name to display the Update Account screen.
  2. On the Upate Account screen, clear the In Use flag.
