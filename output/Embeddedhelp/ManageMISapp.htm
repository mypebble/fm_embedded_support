About the fields
UPN

The unique pupil number allocated to the pupil by the school.

Contacts

Parent/guardian details for the pupil.

Groups

Groups the pupil is linked to, for example, Universal Free School Meals.

Staff

Staff members in charge of the pupil.

Attendance

The pupil attendance record.

Pupil Premium Indicator

The pupil premium funding indicator.

Free school meals

If the pupil receives free school meals.

School name

The school name
