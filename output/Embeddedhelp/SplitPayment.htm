How do I split a payment?

  1. Select the Account from which the Amount is being split.
  2. Enter the Amount you want to split from the original payment.
  3. To continue splitting, click Split Payment and repeat steps 1. and 2. until the Amount Remaining balance is 0 (zero).

    4. Warning! You must continue to record the split payment until the Amount Remaining balance is 0 (zero). If you click Finished before then you will need to cancel the transaction and start again.

  4. When you have finished splitting, click Finished.
