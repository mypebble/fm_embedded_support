About the fields
Transfer From

The account you want to transfer money from.

You can enter a character or characters from the account code in the Search field to display the account.

Transfer To

The account you want to transfer money to.

You can enter a character or characters from the account code in the Search field to display the account.

Amount

The amount of money you want to transfer.

Date

The date of the transfer. This defaults to todayâ€™s date, but you can amend it.

How do I ...

Transfer between two accounts
  1. Select the account you want to transfer money from.
  2. Select the account you want to transfer money to.
  3. Enter the Amount to transfer.
  4. Enter or amend the transfer Date.
  5. Enter any Notes to help you identify the reason for the transfer.

    6. Note: You must enter notes in order to transfer the money.

  6. Click Transfer.
Transfer from the Settlement Account to another account
  • Select the Settlement Account to transfer money from and follow the process for transferring money between two accounts.
