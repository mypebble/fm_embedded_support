About the fields
Period beginning

The start date of the current open period.

Difference

A difference value of 0 (zero) indicates that your balances in your bank accounts match the balances in your user accounts. If the difference value is anything else then you must adjust either your bank account balance (if it is incorrect) or the user account balance, or both to ensure that the balances are correct.

Note: You won’t be able to close a financial period until the Difference is 0 (zero).

Use this screen to check that the money you have in your bank accounts on the left hand side of the screen matches the money which you hold in your user accounts on the right hand side of the screen. If there is a difference you must try to find out why - it may be something as simple as entering an incorrect value in a transaction.

You can amend the opening balances of bank accounts and user accounts but don’t do this as a short-cut to entering a transaction on the system to get your accounts to balance. Entering a transaction - either anew if you had not entered it before, or as a contra-entry to cancel a mistaken entry - is the best way to get your accounts to balance as this provides traceability when you come to audit your accounts.

How do I ...

Deal with a difference?

Note: These are Opening Balances of a closed or locked (draft) period, ready for closedown. Reconciling, paying-in etc will not alter these unless you haven’t fully ‘closed down the period’. In order for you to do any of the following, you would need to cancel the draft period end.

  • Have all bank statements been reconciled for the current period? If so, the difference is probably caused by an entry or missing entry in one or more user accounts.
  • Have you paid into the bank all the cash you have received? If not, then more money may show up in the user accounts than in the bank account.

Tip: Use Reports > Accounts Summary to check the receipts and payments values on the user accounts and use Reports > Search Account Balances to find specific transactions entered on specific accounts.

Amend a bank account balance?
  • Click the hyperlinked Bank Account to display the Update Account screen and follow the instructions there.
Amend a user account balance?
  • Clear the hyperlinked Account to display the Update Account screen and follow the instructions there..
