About the fields
Account

The user account name.

Opening

The account opening balance.

Transfers In

The amount that has been transferred into the account.

Transfers Out

The amount that has been transferred out of the account.

Receipts

The amount representing receipts into the account.

Payments

The amount that has been paid out of the account.

Balance

The account balance. If more money has been paid out of the account in total than has been paid in, this figure will be shown as a negative value.

List of account transactions

The transaction Date, pupil or organisation Name on the transaction, Description, Type, Reference No, amount of Receipt or amount of Payment are shown.

How do I ...

Change the display of the transactions?
  • Click the hyperlink column name to re-order the display of the transactions. For example, clicking on Name will order the display alphabetically. Clicking on Reference No. will order by the number. Clicking on Payment will order by amount paid.
Print transactions list?
  • Click Print. A prompt tells you your report is being prepared. Once it has been prepared it is displayed as a PDF and you can then print the PDF file.
