Amend Paying-in Slip

About the fields

List of cheques

This list of cheques included in the paying-in slip is displayed, showing: Date, user Account, Description, pupil Name, Class and Year, Receipt No and Amount, along with the total number of Cheques to be Paid in and the Total Value of Cheques.

List of Cheques in Hand

A list of all cheques in handA cheque or cheques which you have not yet paid into the bank., or a list of cheques which match characters that are entered in the Filter, is displayed.The list shows the following information for each cheque in hand: Date, user Account, Description, pupil Name, Class and Year, Receipt No and Amount. As you select cheques, the Cheques to be Paid in and Total Value of Cheques amounts are updated alongside the list.

Filter

Enter a character or characters you want to find and the list of displayed cheques in handA cheque or cheques which you have not yet paid into the bank. adjusts accordingly to show a shorter list to choose from.

Note: The filter works by displaying all cheques in hand which contain the character or characters you have entered.

Cash Available to Pay in

The amount of cash that you have available to pay in.

Amount to Pay in

The amount of cash that you are going to pay in.

Total to be Paid in

The sum of the cheques in handA cheque or cheques which you have not yet paid into the bank. which you have selected and the cash amount you are going to pay in.

Bank account

The bank account you are paying the cheques in handA cheque or cheques which you have not yet paid into the bank. and cash into.

Paying-in Slip Number

The number which is linked to the payment. This is displayed automatically and increments sequentially each time you enter a new paying-in slip. As you are amending a paying-in slip you won’t usually amend this, although you might if you had made a mistake in the field when you entered the new paying-in slip.

Date

The date you paid the cheques in hand and cash into the bank.

This is where you record money (cash and cheques) you are paying into the bank.

How do I ...

Amend a paying-in slip?
  1. Select a cheque to add it on to the paying-in slip, or clear a check box to the left of the cheque in hand to omit the cheque from the paying-in slip.
    2. Note:

    You can filter the list that is displaying, using the Filter box.

    You can do this for individual cheques in the list or for all cheques in the list by clicking the box in the column header.

    If you are drawing a cheque for cash, this is not included in the list of cheques in hand. Use Bank & Cash > Draw Cheque for Cash for this.

    The total value of cheques to be paid in is displayed in the box alongside the list of cheques in hand.

  2. The total amount of cash you have available to pay in is shown in Cash Available to Pay in.

    3. Amend the Amount to Pay in if required.

  3. Select the Bank account to pay the cheques and/or cash into.
  4. The Paying-in Slip Number and Date the paying-in slip were filled when the paying-in slip was created.

    5. You can amend these if required but you should not normally need to do this.

  5. Click Amend Paying-in Slip.
Reprint a paying-in slip?
  • Use Bank & Cash > Bank History.
