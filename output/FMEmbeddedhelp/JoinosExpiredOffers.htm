List of Offers

A list of expired offers is displayed, showing: offer Title, Code, Account, Start Dateand End Date.

Filter

Enter a character or characters in the offer you want to find and the list of displayed offers adjusts accordingly to show a shorter list to choose from.

Note: The filter works by displaying all titles or codes which contain the character or characters you have entered.

Use this screen to see what offers have expired.

Note: Offers show up in the expired date if they have been posted and the offer end date has passed.

How do I ...

Find offers?
  • Enter the Title you want to find (if you know it) in the Filter box.
  • Use the Filter to shorten the list of offers displayed, by entering a character or characters you think are in the title.
  • Sort the list of offers by Title, Code, Account, Start Date or End Date by clicking on the hyperlinked column title.
View the offer summary?
  • Click the hyperlinked Title to view the offer summary details.

    • Summary details are the account details and the offer details, including the amount, start and end date, maximum and minimum quantities, items available and whether the offer is recurring. They also list the names of pupils who have access to the offer.

    You can print the summary by clicking Print.
