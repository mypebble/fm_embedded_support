Manage Organisations

About the fields
Filter

Enter a character or characters in the name you want to find and the list of displayed names adjusts accordingly to show a shorter list to choose the name from.

Note: The filter works by displaying all names which contain the character or characters you have entered.

List of Names

A list of organisation Names is displayed. The list shows the organisation Name, Post Code, the date the organisation was Created, and the date the organisation subscription Expires.

Use this screen to add and view organisations, such as local businesses, other schools, etc.

How do I ...

Add a new organisation?
  • Click Create New Organisation to open the Create Organisation window and follow the instructions on that screen.
Find an organisation?
  • Enter the Name you want to find (if you know it) in the Filter box.
  • Use the Filter to shorten the list of names that is displayed, by entering a character or characters you think are in the name.
  • Re-order the list by clicking the hyperlinked column name.
  • If you arenâ€™t sure if the name is out of use, you can show or hide out of use names in the following way:
    • Show out of use names?
      • Click Show out of use.

      Out of use names are shown greyed out in the list.

    • Hide out of use names?
      • Click Hide out of use

      Only in use names are shown in the list.

Amend organisation details?
  • Click the hyperlinked organisation Name to open the Update Organisation screen and follow the instructions on that screen.
Remove organisation details?
  • Clear the In Use check box on the Update Organisation screen and follow the instructions on that screen.
