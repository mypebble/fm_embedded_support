New Claim for Donations

About the fields
Date to

Enter the date to claim gift aid up to.

Preview Donations

Click to view a preview of the donations for the donation period. A list of donations which qualify for gift aid in the period are displayed.

List of Donations

The list of donations made by gift aid donors up to the Date specified, is displayed.

The list shows the Ref, Donor Name, Address, Post Code, Date and Amount of the donation.

This report lets you preview the donations made by designated Gift Aid donors. Gift Aid donors are managed via the Manage Donors link displayed on the screen when you preview the donations for the period or via Administration > Manage Names > Manage Gift Aid Donors.

How do I ...

Preview the list of donations?
  • Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
Print the list of claims?
  1. Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
  2. Click Print.
Export the list of claims?
  1. Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
  2. Click Export to CSV. The list of claims is automatically saved as a download CSVThe name given to a file which stores values that can be read by computer programs. Each line in the file is a record and each record is separated by a comma. file.
Close Donation Period
  1. Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
  2. Click Donation Period.
  3. Read the prompt text that appears before you decide to proceed.

    4. Warning! Once you have closed the period you cannot undo this action.

    • Click Close Gift Aid Period at the prompt which appears.
Backdate a claim for gift aid in a closed period?

If you update the Gift Aid donor details for a transaction that falls in a closed donations period, it will be included automatically in your next submission.
