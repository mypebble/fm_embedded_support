About the fields
Date from

Enter the date from which you want to claim gift aid.

Date to

Enter the date to claim gift aid up to.

Preview Donations

Click to view a preview of the donations for the donation period. A list of donations which qualify for gift aid in the period are displayed.

List of Donations

The list of donations made by gift aid donors up to the Date specified, is displayed.

The list shows the Ref, Donor Name, Address, Post Code, Date and Amount of the donation.

Note: Donors shown in bold are those you can claim for. Those that are greyed out are those for whom you don't have the necessary information to make a claim for. Once you fill in any missing details, they will appear in bold.

This report lets you preview the donations made by designated Gift Aid donors. Gift Aid donors are managed via the Manage Names link displayed on the screen when you preview the donations for the period or via Administration > Manage Names > Update Pupil Details | Gift Aid.

Note: Donors shown in bold are those you can claim for. Those that are greyed out are those for whom you don't have the necessary information to make a claim for. Once you fill in any missing details, they will appear in bold and you will be able to claim for them.

How do I ...

Create a file with less than 1000 entries for HMRC?
  • Use the Date from and Date to boxes to filter the list to less than 1000.
Preview the list of donations?
  1. Enter the date to claim gift aid from.
  2. Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
Print the list of claims?
  1. Enter the date to claim gift aid from.
  2. Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
  3. Click Print.
Export the list of claims?
  1. Enter the date to claim gift aid from.
  2. Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
  3. Click Export to CSV. The list of claims is automatically saved as a download CSVThe name given to a file which stores values that can be read by computer programs. Each line in the file is a record and each record is separated by a comma. file.
  4. Click on the downloaded file to open it.

Note: It is your responsibility to handle personal data in accordance with GDPR law.

Close Donation Period
  1. Enter the date to claim gift aid from.
  2. Enter the date to claim gift aid up to and click Preview Donations.
  3. Click Donation Period.
  4. Read the prompt text that appears before you decide to proceed.

    5. Warning! Once you have closed the period you cannot undo this action.

    • Click Close Gift Aid Period at the prompt which appears.
Backdate a claim for gift aid in a closed period?

If you update the Gift Aid donor details for a transaction that falls in a closed donations period, it will be included automatically in your next submission.
