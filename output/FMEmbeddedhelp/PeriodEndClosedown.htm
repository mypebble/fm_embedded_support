Period End Close-down

About the fields
Period Beginning Date

The start date of the period that you want to close.

Date to

The end date of the period you want to close.

List of Archive Period Reports

A list of reports for previous financial periods is shown: the Account Summary report is the Period End report itself; the Transactions report shows the volume and value of transactions that occurred in the period; Account Balances shows the opening balances, receipts and payments, movement in period and closing balances for all accounts; Bank History shows debits and credits associated with each bank account in the period; Cash History shows debits and credits associated with your Cash In Hand account in the period; Bank Balances shows the opening and closing balances for the period for each of your bank accounts.

Use this screen when you want to produce a Draft period endA preview of what the period end report will show. Producing a draft period end will generate all the reports required for a financial period e.g. Account Summary Income and Expenditure; Transactions; Account Balances; Bank History; Cash History; Closing Bank Balances. ( a preview of the real thing) or are ready to Close a periodThe objectives of closing a period are to: draw a line under your transactions for the period; close any accounts that will not be used in the next period; determine which accounts will have their balances transferred to the new period; balance your income and expenditure for the period with that of your bank.

How do I ...

Produce a draft period end?
  1. Enter the Period Beginning Date and the Date to.
  2. Click Preview.
Close a period?

Ensure that you have completed all the tasks you need to before you close a period.

What do I need to do before I can close a period?

There are preparatory tasks you should do before you close a period:

  • Determine which accounts are to be closed / archived, marked out of use.
  • Clear any closing balances which are not required in the next financial period by using Administration > Transfer between Bank Accounts.
  • Determine which accounts are to be retained with their balance in the next financial period.
  • Reconcile all bank accounts up to or beyond the close date.

    • Note: You must reconcile all bank accounts for the period, even bank accounts that have no transactions.

  • Print a list of cancelled transactions.
  • Check that your bank and user account balances match, using Administration > View Opening Balances.

For more information see Period End close-down.
