How do I split a receipt?

  1. Select the Account from which the Amount is being split.
  2. Select the Name the Amount is to be recorded against.
  3. Enter the Amount you want to split from the original receipt.
  4. To continue splitting, click Split Receipt and repeat steps 1. to 3. until the Amount Remaining balance is 0 (zero).

    5. Warning! You must continue to record the split receipt until the Amount Remaining balance is 0 (zero). If you click Finished before then you will need to cancel the transaction and start again.

  5. When you have finished splitting, click Finished.
