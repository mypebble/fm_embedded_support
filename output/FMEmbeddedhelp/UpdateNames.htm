Updated Names

About the fields
Date from

The start date of changes to the pupil records.

Date to

The end date for the changes to the pupil records.

New

See new records which were added between the Date from and Date to dates. This includes contacts which have been added automatically via MISapp > Import Names and those that have been added manually using Administration > Manage Names > Create New Name.

Deleted

See records which were deleted between the Date from and Date to dates. This includes all contacts that still appear in Fund Manager but have been removed from Sims (or other management tool) and are no longer being updated as part of the import. For example, the names of students or staff who have left.

Changed

See records which changed between the Date from and Date to dates.

Changed Schools

Note: This only applies to schools in North Tyneside.

See records for pupils who have changed schools between the Date from and Date to dates.

List of Names

A list of names is shown for any records that are New, Deleted, Changed, or Changed Schools during the Date from and Date to dates.

Filter

Enter a character or characters in the name you want to find and the list of displayed names adjusts accordingly to show a shorter list to choose the name from.

Note: The filter works by displaying all names which contain the character or characters you have entered.

Use this screen to see the pupil records that have been updated over a period of time. A list of names is shown for each type of change - new, deleted, changed or changed schools.

How do I ...

Add new names?
  • Use Administration > Manage Names > Create New Name.
View new names?
  1. Enter dates to search between.
  2. Click Search.
  3. Click New to display the New Names recorded between the dates.

    4. The New Names list shows when the pupil record was Created, which Class, Year, Registration group the pupil has been assigned to and the Status of the pupil record - whether it is in use or not.

    Note: In order to see pupil records that are out of use, you must click Show out of use.

    You can:

    • Show or hide pupil records that are out of use, by clicking the Show out of use/Hide out of use button.
    • Use the Filter to filter the list.
    • Order the names differently, by clicking the hyperlinked column heading Name.
    • Drill down to see pupil details by clicking the hyperlinked name to open the Update Pupil screen.
    • Drill down to see who else is in the Class, Year, Reg or Groups, by clicking the hyperlinked class, year, registration class or group.
      • From the Class, Year, Reg or Groups lists, you can:
        • Click a pupil Name to update the details of a pupil in the class.
        • Show or hide records that are out of use.
        • See which pupil records have Gift Aid Donor selected.
        • Print the list of names.

Delete pupil records?

Note: Deleted records are those that still appear in Fund Manager but have been removed from Sims (or other management tool) and are no longer being updated as part of the import.

  • You cannot manually delete pupil records in Fund Manager. Instead they should be marked as out of use within Fund Manager.
View deleted names?
  1. Enter dates to search between.
  2. Click Search.
  3. Click Deleted to display the Deleted Names recorded between the dates.

    4. The Deleted Names list shows when the pupil record was Deleted, which Class, Year, Registration class and Groups the pupil has been assigned to and the Status of the pupil record - whether it is in use or not.

    Note: In order to see pupil records that are out of use, you must click Show out of use.

    You can:

    • Show or hide pupil records that are out of use, by clicking the Show out of use/Hide out of use button.
    • Use the Filter to filter the list.
    • Order the names differently, by clicking the hyperlinked column heading Name.
    • Drill down to see pupil details by clicking the hyperlinked name to open the Update Pupil screen.
    • Mark the pupil names as out of use by selecting the names and clicking Mark name(s) as out of use.

Update pupil details
  • Drilldown on the Name to open the Update Pupil screen and follow the instructions on that screen.
View changed pupil records?

Note: Updates to any information on a pupil record will cause a contact to appear within the Changed Names list. This includes changes to Forename, Surname, Year, Class, Reg Group, Contact Name, Contact Address etc. It is not currently possible to see the specific information that has changed.

  1. Enter dates to search between.
  2. Click Search.
  3. Click Changed to display the Changed Names recorded between the dates.

    4. The Changed Names list shows when the pupil record was Changed, which Class, Year, Registration group and Groups the pupil has been assigned to and the Status of the pupil record - whether it is in use or not.

    Note: In order to see include pupil records that are out of use, you must click Show out of use.

    You can:

    • Show or hide pupil records that are out of use, by clicking the Show out of use/Hide out of use button.
    • Use the Filter to filter the list.
    • Order the names differently, by clicking the hyperlinked column heading Name.
    • Drill down to see pupil details by clicking the hyperlinked name to open the Update Pupil screen.
    • Drill down to see who else is in the Class, Year, Reg or Groups, by clicking the hyperlinked class, year, registration class or group.
      • From the Class, Year, Reg or Groups lists, you can:
        • Click a pupil Name to update the details of a pupil in the class.
        • Show or hide records that are out of use.
        • See which pupil records have Gift Aid Donor selected.
        • Print the list of names.

View details of pupils who have moved schools?

Note: This only applies to schools in North Tyneside.

Information from MISapp is pulled and displayed for pupils who have changed schools between the dates specified.

  1. Enter dates to search between.
  2. Click Search.
  3. Click Changed Schools to display the list of Moved Names.

    4. The Moved Names list shows when the pupil Moved, their Old School and their Current School.

    You can:

    • Show or hide pupil records that are out of use, by clicking the Show out of use/Hide out of use button.
    • Use the Filter to filter the list.
    • Order the names differently, by clicking the hyperlinked column heading Name.
    • Drill down to see pupil details by clicking the hyperlinked name.
